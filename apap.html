Brand Overview

The Popular Association of Savings and Loans (APAP) is a private financial institution, of a mutualist nature, created under the Law No. 5897, of May 14, 1962, with the objective of promoting savings for the financing of the purchase, construction and/or improvement of the family home.

Beyond its traditional leadership in financing affordable and middle-class housing, and as part of its diversification strategy, APAP has extended its support to the most important productive sectors of the country, consolidating its participation in the Dominican financial system.
